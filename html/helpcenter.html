Payment

What payment methods are accepted on Soscom?

❯

We accept a variety of payment methods, including:
  • Pay on Delivery allows for payment in cash or with credit/debit cards, or Mobile Money via Soscom Post upon delivery.
  • SoscomPay accepts payment through Mastercard, Visa, or Mobile Money.
  • Vouchers allow you to pay using a voucher code.
  • Bank Deposit/Transfer

Payment

How secured is my payment Information on soscom?

❯

SoscomPay is committed to use the best commercially available technology and procedures to protect the security of your online transactions. To secure your transaction, you have 2 levels of control:
  • Your Soscom password
  • Your one-time verification code sent by your bank to your phone.

Payment

What do i do if my payment is declined?

❯

This could have happened because:
  • You attempted to pay via Debit/Credit Card but wrongly entered the Security PIN / Name / Expiry date or your card is not activated for online transactions by your bank. We suggest you select another payment method next time or call your bank to enable your card for future transactions.
  • You did not update OTP sent as an SMS by the Bank. Sometimes the phone number registered with your card or bank account is different than the one you are using at the time of your transaction. Please call your bank to update to the correct phone number or use the SIM card associated with your bank account to get the OTP.
  • You have insufficient funds/credit. In this case, select another payment method or make a transaction after you have sufficient funds/credit on your card/bank account.
  • The bank has declined to process this transaction.
If any amount was debited from your mobile money/bank account, please Chat With Us and provide us with the details of the transaction.
Note: SoscomPay and Soscom will never ask you for your password, PIN, CVV or full card details over the phone or via email.

Can i pay cash on delivery for my orders?

❯

Yes, you can pay for your orders in cash upon delivery. You can now also use SoscomPay on delivery for all your pay-on-delivery orders.
Yes, you heard right! You can now pay with your card, Mobile Money or USSD upon delivery via the SoscomPay post.

Payment

What should i do if i have been chrged twice for the same order?

❯

If you have been charged twice for the same order, kindly check your bank statement to validate if a reversal has been done. If you are yet to see the value in your account, kindly reach out to us via our Live Chat channel.
Please provide the following information to Soscom customer service when reporting a double charge:
  • Your name and email address used to place the order.
  • Order number and date of purchase.
  • Details of the double charge (amount, date, and transaction number).
Our customer service will assist you in resolving the issue and arranging for a refund if applicable.

How long does it take for my payment to be processed?

❯

Most payments on Soscom are processed immediately when completed. However, specific processing times may vary depending on the payment method.
To ensure that your payment is processed quickly, we recommend that you double-check all the information before completing the checkout. If you are experiencing an issue with your payment, please contact our customer service team for further assistance.

What is Soscom Store credit?How do I use it?

❯

Your Soscom Store Credit can be acquired through refunds due to cancellations or returns or through various rewards and Top-ups. You can use this store credit to pay for your orders on Soscom and SoscomPay platforms, given that certain conditions are met. To view your existing store credits, go to your Soscom account and click on the 'Account' tab, or log in to SoscomPay to check your balance.
